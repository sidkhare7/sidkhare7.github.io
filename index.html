On-Chain Reputation Engine, Zeno Labs

Reputation Systems Solidity Rust DeFi

02
Abracast: AI-Resolved Prediction Market

Prediction market that resolves outcomes via an on-chain LLM oracle with no human dispute resolution. Explores which questions AI can reliably answer. Bribery-resistant by design. Deployed on Avalanche Fuji.

Prediction Markets Solidity Avalanche LLM Oracle
IC3 Blockchain Camp 2026
03
SoK: Fraud Detection on Ethereum

Systemization of different GNN pipelines classifying fraudulent Ethereum wallets: phishing scammers, hackers, wash traders from on-chain behavioral and temporal signals. Analyzed HGATE architecture with contrastive self-supervised learning.

Graph ML GNN Ethereum PyTorch

Pipeline on AWS Redshift tracking AI adoption across firms via millions of job postings. Fine-tuned transformers for job classification. VC network analysis over 100k+ entities.
