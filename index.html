Applied Projects
On-Chain Reputation Engine — Zeno Labs

Scores DeFi wallets by z-normalised on-chain behavior — holding duration, value, volume — weighted by market cap. High-reputation wallets gate community access. Sybil-resistant by design. Built in Rust and Solidity.

Reputation Systems Solidity Rust DeFi
$10k grant from Northeastern IDEA Ventures · Zeno Labs, 2025
Abracast — AI-Resolved Prediction Market

Prediction market that resolves outcomes via an on-chain LLM oracle — no human dispute resolution. Explores which questions AI can reliably answer. Bribery-resistant by design. Deployed on Avalanche Fuji.

Prediction Markets Solidity Avalanche LLM Oracle
IC3 Blockchain Camp 2026 Hackathon · Princeton · Team: Sofia Bonazzola (Yale), marwamou (Technion)
SoK: Fraud Detection on Ethereum

GNN pipeline classifying fraudulent Ethereum wallets — phishing scammers, hackers, wash traders — from on-chain behavioral and temporal signals. Built on the HGATE architecture with contrastive self-supervised learning.

Graph ML GNN Ethereum PyTorch
Submitted to SBC Stanford 2026 · Advisor: Prof. Tej Anand, UMD
AIMaps.ai — AI Labor Market Intelligence

Pipeline on AWS Redshift tracking AI adoption across firms via millions of job postings. Fine-tuned transformers for job classification. VC network analysis over 100k+ entities.

Transformers AWS Redshift NetworkX
Advisor: Prof. Anil Gupta, UMD · 2023
